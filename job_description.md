# Job Description: Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team

**Company:** Redrob AI (Series A AI-native talent intelligence platform)
**Location:** Pune/Noida, India (Hybrid) | Open to relocation from Tier-1 Indian cities
**Experience:** 5–9 years

## What You'd Actually Be Doing
Own the intelligence layer of Redrob's product — the ranking, retrieval, and matching
systems that decide what recruiters see when they search for candidates.

## Things You Absolutely Need
- Production experience with embeddings-based retrieval systems (sentence-transformers,
  BGE, E5, or similar) deployed to real users
- Production experience with vector databases or hybrid search infrastructure —
  Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, FAISS, Elasticsearch, or similar
- Strong Python. Code quality matters.
- Hands-on experience designing evaluation frameworks for ranking systems —
  NDCG, MRR, MAP, offline-to-online correlation, A/B test interpretation

## Things We'd Like You to Have
- LLM fine-tuning experience (LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT)
- Experience with learning-to-rank models
- Prior exposure to HR-tech or marketplace products
- Open-source contributions in the AI/ML space

## Explicit Disqualifiers
- Entire career at consulting firms (TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Accenture, Cognizant, etc.)
- AI experience consisting primarily of recent LangChain/OpenAI wrapper projects
- Primary expertise in computer vision, speech, or robotics without NLP/IR exposure
- Pure research background with no production deployment

## Ideal Candidate
6–8 years total, 4–5 in applied ML at product companies. Has shipped at least one
end-to-end ranking, search, or recommendation system to real users. Located in or
willing to relocate to Noida or Pune.
